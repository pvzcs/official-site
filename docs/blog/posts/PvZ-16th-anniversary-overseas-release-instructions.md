# 关于PvZ16周年庆典海外发布的相关说明

> 外文标题 / TITLE：Relevant Instructions for the Overseas Release of PvZ 16th Anniversary Celebration

## 原文 / CHINESE VERSION

根据庆典筹备前期所定立的合作关系，原主办PvZ同人创作论坛、PvZ同人创作者协会（现已变更为植创社）和PvZ 1 Modders Association达成了合作关系，海外征稿、发布交由PvZ 1 Modders Association负责。

本社团继续承认上述合作关系，因此，Youtube账号PvZ 1 ​Modders Association为本社团所认可的海外发布账号。

我们在此感谢各位国内外创作者和观众对本次活动的支持。

## 译文 / ENGLISH VERSION

According to the cooperation relationship established in the early stage of the celebration preparation, the original organizers, namely the PvZHome, and PvZers Associations (now transformed as the Zhi Chuang She), and the PvZ 1 Modders Association, have reached a cooperation agreement. The solicitation of contributions from overseas and the release of relevant works are entrusted to the PvZ 1 Modders Association.

We (Zhi Chuang She) continues to recognize the above-mentioned cooperation relationship. Therefore, the YouTube account of PvZ 1 Modders Association is the overseas release account recognized by our association.

We would like to express our gratitude to all the creators and audiences both at home and abroad for their support of this event.
